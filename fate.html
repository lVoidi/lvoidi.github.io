God's final warning

Space has a beginning, but no end. —Infinite.
Stars also have a beginning, but meet and end of their own making. —Finite.
History has shown that those with wisdom are the greatest fools of all.
The fish that live in the sea know not of the land.
If they had wisdom, they too would be destroyed.
It is funnier that man shall exceed the speed of light than for fish to start living on land.
This can be called God's final warning to those who can resist.

Philosophers have only interpreted the world, the point is to change it.

Don't turn a blind eye to the problems around you.

Be loud.

Be annoying.

Be revolutionary.

Or else, history will make fun of you for embracing status quo.
