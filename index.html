Welcome To The Machine

The world is heading torwards a Technological Singularity, but is it really a bad thing? We always talk about robots taking over humanity, but that will not happen. We will be unified with them. It is already happening. Neuralink, LLM that do most of the work for us, large scale algorithms that predict very accurately our train of though. We are already getting unified with them.

People do not seem to be aware of the changes that are evolving mankind. Quantum chips, LLM evolving at an exponential rate, as seem by the AGI index for the OpenAI models such as o1, o3 tuned high, and recent releases of Deepseek, etc.

In this website, i will present you my ideas, thoughs and reflections about the world we live in, technology, and every aspect related to computers and life.

Disclaimer: This website is designed to be seen in a desktop browser. You can read articles in mobile, but algorithms visualizations might be broken on mobile.
