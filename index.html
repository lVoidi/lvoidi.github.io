About me

My name is Rodrigo, I am a hobbyist programmer, linux advocate and science enthusiast, currently studying Computer Engineering in the Costa Rica Institute of Technology. Cutting edge technologies like blockchain, AI and robotics really pique my interest. I love engineering and everything it involves. Additionally, I am a music lover and I play the bass. Click my picture to check out my Github.

I'm more of an engineering kind of guy. I wanted to be some kind of mad scientist when I was a kid and watched a lot of documentaries about space, black holes, quantum physics, even though I didn't understand a thing!

This let me feed the biggest monster inside me: curiosity. I always wanted to understand how things work, and well, that induced me to study computer engineering. I went to a scientific high school here in Costa Rica, a well known system of high schools that focus on science and engineering. I learned a lot of math, physics, biology and chemistry there. While I was there, I was learning programming by myself as a hobby, and I got really into it.

Never really created a life changing project during this period, it was always just accumulating a huge amount of knowledge and, over all, logical thinking. I always liked to solve problems, and programming is all about that.

Now here I am. My whole career is now based on it! However, computer engineering is a kind of broad, broad career. I would risk to say that it is 1/3 programming and 2/3 electronics. It is more focused on understanding computers, not just programming them.

Now, I am in my second year of college, out of five. I have made lots of projects, i participated in a hackathon and a CTF. I have built a lot of interesting projects and maybe my mad scientist self does exist! All of this, led me to build this website. I am not really good at web development, even, I asked AI about a lot of stuff and I'm not afraid of saying it! I just wanted a place to showcase my ideas. Again, this isn't a skill showcase, I don't have to showcase anything or say that i can do some stuff. I prove that with my projects! This is a place to order my thoughts and even share them with people.

So, having said that, welcome! Hope you can find something interesting here. Feel free to contact me, send feedback, or whatever!
